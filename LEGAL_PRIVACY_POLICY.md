# Privacy Policy

**Affina Space** · Effective date: [18 July 2026] · Draft for legal review

This policy explains what personal data we collect when you use Affina Space
(the "Service") — our platform for women founders at affina.space and
app.affina.space — how we use it, and the rights you have over it. We've
written it in plain language on purpose: transparency is part of the product.

## 1. What we collect

**Account & profile data.** Your email address (verified via a sign-in link),
name, your project's name, and — if you choose to share it — your phone
number, country, city and timezone.

**Your venture content.** The Service is built around the work you do in it:
your answers about your idea, customers and business model, your goals,
exercise answers, notes from customer interviews you record in the platform,
weekly check-ins, and topics you submit when requesting a mentor session. We
call this "User Content". It can include personal reflections you choose to
write (for example, why your project matters to you).

**Usage & analytics data.** We run our **own, first-party analytics** — we do
not use third-party advertising trackers or pixels. We record product events
(pages viewed, steps completed, features used) linked to a random identifier
stored in your browser, and — if you arrived via a marketing link — the
campaign parameters (UTM tags) of your first and most recent visit. We do
**not** store your IP address or device fingerprint in our analytics events.

**Payment data.** Payments are processed by Stripe. We never receive or store
your card details. We store your Stripe customer reference, your subscription
status and billing period dates.

**Email interaction data.** We log which emails we have sent you (type and
time) to avoid duplicates and understand deliverability.

## 2. How we use your data

- **To provide the Service:** run your program, generate your reports and
  feedback, save your progress, operate mentor sessions and community
  features.
- **AI-generated guidance:** your User Content is processed by our AI
  provider (see §4) to generate feedback, scores, reports and suggestions
  about **your** project. This is the core of the Service.
- **To communicate with you:** sign-in links, receipts and account messages;
  program emails (weekly tasks, reflection prompts, reminders); and, with
  your consent or as permitted by law, occasional product updates and useful
  content. You can opt out of non-essential emails at any time (see §9).
- **To improve the Service:** aggregated, first-party analytics about how the
  product is used, and attribution of sign-ups to marketing campaigns.
- **To protect the Service:** rate limiting, abuse prevention and security
  (these systems may process your IP address transiently).

**Legal bases (GDPR):** performance of a contract (providing the Service),
legitimate interests (first-party analytics, service emails, security),
consent (where required — e.g. optional phone contact, marketing
communications), and legal obligations (tax/accounting records for payments).

## 3. What we do NOT do

- We do not sell your personal data.
- We do not run third-party advertising trackers on the Service.
- We do not use your User Content to advertise to others.
- Your venture ideas remain yours (see the Terms — you own your User
  Content).

Feedback, scores and reports in Affina are generated with the help of large
language models (Anthropic's Claude). Your relevant User Content is sent to
Anthropic's API strictly to produce your outputs and is handled under our
agreement with Anthropic; we do not permit its use for training their models

## 6. Cookies & local storage

We keep this minimal and functional:
- **Session cookie** (`affina_session`) — keeps you signed in for up to 30
  days. Essential; httpOnly and secure.
- **Local storage** — a local cache of your workspace, a random analytics
  identifier, and small flags (e.g. "this popup was already shown").
We do not set third-party cookies. [Lawyer: confirm whether the first-party

## 7. Retention

- Account data and User Content: for as long as your account exists.
- If you ask us to delete your account, we delete your personal data and User
  Content within 30 days, except records we must keep by law (e.g. invoices).
- Email logs and analytics events: kept while the account exists, then
  deleted or irreversibly anonymised.
- Pending sign-ups (email captured but never verified) that stay inactive may
  be deleted after 12 months.

## 8. Your rights

Under the GDPR (and similar laws), you can: access a copy of your data;
correct it; delete it; receive it in a portable format; object to or restrict
certain processing; and withdraw consent at any time where processing is
based on consent. Write to **sk@affina.space** — we respond within 30 days.
You can also lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority (in France:
the CNIL).

## 9. Marketing communications

Program emails (e.g. your weekly tasks and reflection prompts) are part of
the Service. Non-essential emails (product news, useful content) are sent
with your consent or under legitimate interest, and every such email includes
an unsubscribe link. Opting out never affects your access to the Service.

## 10. Security

Access to your data is protected by signed, httpOnly session cookies;
sign-in uses one-time email links (no passwords to leak); data is encrypted
in transit; production access is restricted; payment card data never touches
our systems. No system is perfectly secure — if we learn of a breach
affecting your data, we will notify you and the authorities as required by
law.

## 11. Who the Service is for

Affina is for adults — you must be 18 or older. We do not knowingly collect
data from children.

## 12. Changes

If we materially change this policy, we'll notify you by email or in the
product before the change takes effect.
